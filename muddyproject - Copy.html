FAQs

Does Muddy replace Chrome?

  • Most users opens apps and websites directly in muddy and use it as their default browser for work. We make it easy to import your history, passwords, and logins too.

Can i use Muddy by myself?

  • Muddy is design to make work easy for teamwork but spaces can be powerful for individuals too. You can build out a workspace by yourself first before sharing with others.

Do i have to connect my app to use Muddy's AI ?

  • There's no setup required. Out of the box, Muddy's AI can start browsing the web for you to help answer questions. As you create projects in Muddy, it will automatically use that context to help answer questions and retrieve websites.

What apps does Muddy support?

  • Anything that works inside of your browser today, including Chrome extensions.

How does Muddy manage my privacy?

  • All your cookies and passwords are stored locally and never on our server. Our business model is around selling collaboration services and enterprise features, not ads.

Does opening all those apps make my computer slow?

  • Muddy takes care of app and tab management for you. Memory and battery life is optimized by hibernating old tabs automatically and waking them up when needed.
